Organic meditative ambient score for an immersive biophilic charity launch experience at dusk. Deep forest atmosphere, warm canopy glow, living moss walls, soft water movement, leaves and breath-like textures, hopeful emotional lift near the final product reveal. Tempo 62 BPM, spacious, seamless loop, cinematic ambient, organic electronic, warm but not sentimental.

## What To Do After Suno Generates It

1. Download the best version from Suno as an MP3.
2. Rename it to `background-music.mp3`.
3. Put it in the website's `assets` folder, so the final path is `assets/background-music.mp3`.
4. Open `cotton-on-foundation-launch-experience.html` and press the Sound button. The site will use that MP3 automatically; if the file is missing, it falls back to the built-in generative ambient soundscape.
